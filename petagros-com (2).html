Who We Are

Moldova's leading
PET preform enterprise

Petagros-Com S.R.L. is a Moldovan company specialising exclusively in the production of PET preforms — the foundational component of plastic packaging for the beverage and food industries.

In 2002, we built the first modern PET preform manufacturing facility in Moldova. Since then, we have grown into the country's leading producer, investing continuously in capacity, quality, and technology.

Headquartered in Ungheni, Moldova, our facility operates with advanced injection moulding lines and serves clients across the beverage, brewing, and spirits sectors.

Our investment program — exceeding $2 million USD — has expanded annual production from 156 million to 300 million preforms, with a development roadmap targeting 600 million units per year.

2002
Moldova's First Modern Preform Factory Company founded and first-of-its-kind PET preform plant constructed in Ungheni.
2004
First Capacity Expansion Production reaches 156 million preforms per year; investment project for second line initiated.
2005
Second Line Launch Second production line commissioned in December, doubling output to 300 million preforms per year.
2006
Roadmap: 600M Units Plans filed for two additional production lines, targeting 600 million preforms annually.
